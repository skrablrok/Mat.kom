services

Production and Installation of Metal Structures

We offer comprehensive services in the production and installation of metal structures, from simple to more demanding ones. Our structures are manufactured according to client drawings and specifications, ensuring precision and high quality. Services include mechanical processing, material cutting, and various types of surface treatments such as sandblasting, powder coating, galvanizing, and painting.

Mechanical Stages and Theatre Machines for Theatres and Cruise Ships

We specialize in the design, installation, and servicing of mechanical stages and advanced theatre machines for use in theatres, concert halls, and cruise ships. Our solutions are tailored to the specific needs of clients and ensure reliable and safe operation of stage mechanisms in diverse environments.

5-Axis Abrasive Water Jet Cutting

We provide high-quality 5-axis abrasive water jet cutting, offering exceptionally precise cutting of nearly any material. This advanced technology allows for complex shapes to be cut without thermal deformation, as no high temperatures are generated during the process.

Production and Installation of Gates, Fences, and Canopies

Our offer includes planning, material selection, custom manufacturing, and final installation, all carried out with high precision, durability, and aesthetic excellence.
